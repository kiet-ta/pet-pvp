## **Story Workshop**
  ### _**1. Story ideation Techniques**_
  **1.1 Game Type and Setting**
  - Determine the genre of your game (e.g., arcade vs. role-playing) to guide the story's direction.
  - Establish the setting (e.g., futuristic space or a cartoon world) to inform the narrative context.

  **1.2 Character and Goal Development**
  - Start with the hero's goal, which serves as the "spine" of the story, and explore the reasons behind this goal.
  - Use a series of "why" questions to deepen the understanding of the hero's motivations and emotional connections.

  **1.3 Antagonist and World-Building**
  - Define the antagonist's goals and motivations to enrich the conflict within the story.
  - Describe the game world in detail, creating a vivid backdrop that enhances the narrative and gameplay experience.

  ### _**2. Personal Writing Advise**_
  **1. Writing Process and challenges**
  - The author expresses the emotional struggle of writing, describing it as both enjoyable and anxiety-inducing.
  - They emphasize the importance of finding a comfortable mental space to foster creativity and productivity.
